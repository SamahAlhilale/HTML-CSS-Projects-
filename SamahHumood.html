Shopping Habits 

In recent years, shopping habits have undergone significant changes due to various factors and advancements in technology.
       With the rise of e-commerce and online shopping platforms, consumers now have access to a vast array of products and services
        from the comfort of their homes. This convenience has led to a shift towards online shopping, 
      with a growing number of people opting to make purchases online rather than visiting physical stores.
